## Context สำคัญ

**keygen output format:**
```csv
jd,date_ce,su,mo,ma,me,ju,ve,sa,ra,ke,mr
730428,2000-01-01,287.1234,...
```

**scraper output format (JSONL):**
```json
{"jd":715323,"name":"Thaksin Shinawatra","event_label":"นายกรัฐมนตรี","type":"human","lat":null,"lng":null,"time_utc":null,"relate_id":[],"source":"wikipedia:en:Thaksin_Shinawatra","confidence":0.95,"notes":null}
```

**engine.js planet order:** sp[1..10] = SU MO MA ME JU VE SA RA KE MR
**degrees convert:** `sp[i] / 60` (21600/circle ÷ 60 = 360°)
